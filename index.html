Developer Student Club

UUM

Google Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Learn more

Our Purpose

Mission

To teach the public about knowledge, skills, and the impact of technology in current era.

Vision

To create a community of student who is passionate in technology and wish to help the society.

Values

Take your tools, take your ideas, and create something that will inspire the world.

What We Do

WORKSHOPS

Various of workshops organized by GDSC UUM for those who have interest in technology to enhance their technical skills and knowledge. All of our programs are open to public so be sure to check it out.

TALKS

We invited experienced and professional speaker for giving a talk about anything, from technology knowledge, current trend to leadership skills. Learning from an expert is like a 'shortcut' for learning something new.

COMPETITIONS

Various competition organized by GDSC UUM for local and international university students to compete and socialize between each other. A lot of amazing prize for the winner and also swags for all the participants, so what are you waiting for. Talent without registration is just nothing, come and join our competition!

DESIGN

We have expertises to teach various of design software and design concept. Design basically not an art, as we focus on user interface and user experience with some psychology tricks. Not sure how to start a design? No experience using Figma, Teta etc? No worry, we got your back.
